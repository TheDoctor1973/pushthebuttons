So how does all this work?

Users that have already racked up enough pushes to unlock the 'PTB Shareholder' activity will be eligible to start the 'On Launch' activity.

Once PTB has officially launched, these users will have their conditions changed in preparation for Shareholder status.

Users at this level will only get button pushes added to their count for 'NEW' or 'PUSH+' buttons and they will have to be logged in for the count to be secure. All other click events and buttons no longer count towards the push total.

Logged in users will appear on the Leaderboard page:

Also, once the count active buttons are pushed, the activity, challenge or game will only be able to be completed once every 24 hours. A lockdown timer will stay in place for 24 hours and then re-open these buttons.

This creates a more purposeful, competitive arena in which the Leaderboard can be regulated. Imagine the pressure of only having one chance a day to get as many button pushes from a single opportunity such as the Clumsy Bird Challenge. Remember these activities and others like it can be replayed as many times as you like, but only the first playthrough of the 24 hour cycle counts!
