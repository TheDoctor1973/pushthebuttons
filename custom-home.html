PTB is PWA

Push the Buttons is a fully functional Progressive Web App (PWA), which means you can access content offline just like an app downloaded from the app store.

Installing to your device powers up your experience and also means you can see when 'NEW' buttons are released thanks to the Web Badging and push notification APIs:

The Personal Protection page has 3 standalone PWA apps that can be installed to your device for quick and easy access, just look for the install prompt when you launch them.

These apps are crucial to your personal safety.

Defender, Yellow Beacon and Emergency.
