Personal Protection

Protect yourself and/or help others with these Progressive Web Apps (PWAs).

The Defender App creates a convincing screen showing that Police have been alerted and are 2 minutes away.

This is a preventative or pre-emptive action designed or having the power to deter or prevent an anticipated situation or occurrence.

How Defender works

Defender is useful for home invasions or when your out and about. Designed to make your antagonist panic and retreat.

For example, if your in a dangerous situation where you are unable to call Police or Emergency, or for some reason are unable to speak or communicate, then showing your antogonist the Defender screen would confuse them and they would assume that this app somehow has sensed danger and notified the Police.

Alternatively for a home invasion, you might not want to confront them incase they are armed or on drugs, so the action would be ring Police first then set the desktop or TV screen to Defender mode. This is if you hear them breaking in and have time to do this (would work best with Google Home voice command for example) otherwise your phone Defender screen is Plan B.

This would also be effective to be integrated into your home alarm system when you're not home as an added deterrent.

*Important - installing to your device makes Defender work offline, much more streamlined and easier to access.
