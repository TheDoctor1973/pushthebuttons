Push the Buttons is a Progressive Web App (PWA) where users can earn money and uncover useful, hopeful, practical and random content.

We like to think that PTB has content that can help people, to make their lives just a little bit more enriched.

There are incentives for increasing your push totals and level up to access and unlock extra content and other rewards.

Some content is derived from talented people at CodePen whereas content like Defender, Yellow Beacon and many others are exclusive to PTB.

PTB works in 2 phases, Pre-Launch and Post-Launch.

Pre-Launch is for casual users engaging the activities/apps/challenges and checking back for NEW activities.

Post-Launch takes it to the next level and users compete for Leaderboard position for the chance to earn rewards.

Now get going and Push the Buttons!
